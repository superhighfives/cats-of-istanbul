Cats of Istanbul

In August 2013, I went to Istanbul

While there, I saw cats. Loads and loads of them. There were big ones and little ones and old ones and sick ones and funny ones. There were on every street, in every corner. We asked our tour guide, and she said they had always been there, and that they made people happy.

So over the next five days I took photos of them.

These are the cats of Istanbul.
